About

What Viridian does

Take a photo of a Pokémon card and Viridian tells you which card it is, roughly what grade it might get, and what it's worth — in a few seconds, free, with no account.

In plain terms

You upload a picture. Viridian finds the card in the photo, measures how well-centred it is, estimates the condition of its corners, edges and surface, works out exactly which card it is, and shows what it's worth — both raw and if it were professionally graded.

How accurate it is

Centring is measured — a real geometric reading of the borders against PSA's tolerances. Corners, edges, surface and the graded value are estimates meant to give you a ballpark. Treat Viridian as a quick second opinion to help you decide which cards are worth sending to a professional grader like PSA or ACE — not a replacement for one.

What it's built on

  • TCGdex — open card database & live market prices
  • pokemon-cards-css — the holographic card effect (by simeydotme)
  • OpenCV — the computer vision behind the grading

Viridian is an estimation tool for personal use. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to PSA, ACE, or any grading company. Pokémon card names and images are © their respective owners (Nintendo · Creatures · GAME FREAK). Always confirm high-value cards with a professional grading service.
